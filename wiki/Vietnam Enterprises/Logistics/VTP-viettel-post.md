## Business Description
Viettel Post is Vietnam's leading parcel and last-mile delivery operator, leveraging the nationwide retail footprint of parent Viettel Group. Revenue concentrates in B2C e-commerce parcels, B2B contract logistics, and a growing cross-border lane via partnerships with global integrators. The company has been transitioning from a pure asset-heavy parcel network toward a tech-enabled fulfillment and digital commerce platform.

## Recent Catalysts
