# Seafood Export

**One-liner:** Vietnam's processed-fish export industry — a top
agri-export pillar targeting US$12bn in 2026 and US$15–20bn medium-term.

## What it is

[[vasep-12-billion-seafood-2026]] documents the headline indicators:
Jan–Apr 2026 exports = US$3.7bn (+15% YoY), with China at 26.7% share
and +52.3% YoY, Japan at 13.8% (+1.8%), US at 13% (-7.4% — the only
declining major market).

## How it works

The pipeline links four nodes: capture/aquaculture → processing → cold
chain → import market. VASEP's policy programme covers each: broodstock
quality and disease control upstream, labour stabilisation and capital
access in processing, port and traceability infrastructure between, and
economic diplomacy + trade-defence support at the destination.

## Where it shows up

- In [[topics/vietnam-trade-investment]] as a top export-pillar component
- In [[iuu-fishing]] as the regulated activity being gated
- In [[trade-defense]] as the most-investigated export category

- Whether processing-labour shortages can be solved without curtailing
