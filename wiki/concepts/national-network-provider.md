# National Network Provider

**One-liner:** A designated domestic logistics partner that handles a
foreign carrier's in-country pickup, delivery, warehousing, and customs
coordination — the contractual pattern of the FedEx–Viettel Post 2026
deal.

## What it is

Effective 26 Apr 2026, Viettel Post serves as FedEx's National Network
Provider in Vietnam ([[fedex-viettel-post-partnership]]). The model
defines a clean split of responsibilities: domestic ops belong to the
NNP; international transport, service standards, and digital solutions
belong to the foreign carrier.

## How it works

NNP arrangements typically encompass: pickup and delivery (the last-mile
fleet), warehouse operations, and customs clearance coordination. The
foreign carrier owns the air/ocean network, end-to-end SLAs, and
digital-product layer. Coordination interfaces (shipment events, label
formats, customs data) need to be aligned for the integration to feel
seamless to customers.
