# Cross-border E-commerce

**One-liner:** Online retail flows that originate and terminate in
different customs territories — Vietnam's fastest-growing logistics
demand driver in 2026.

## What it is

Per [[fedex-viettel-post-partnership]], cross-border e-commerce is the
explicit growth thesis of the FedEx–Viettel Post deal. SMEs in particular
benefit from broader coverage, earlier deliveries, and extended pickup
cut-offs; even small timing improvements translate into inventory and
responsiveness gains.

## How it works

A cross-border e-commerce flow stacks: marketplace order → domestic
pickup → export customs → international transit → import customs →
domestic last-mile. Each handover is a potential friction point;
end-to-end visibility and standardised handovers (the FedEx–Viettel Post
contract's main promise) are how providers compete.
