# Supply Chain Visibility

**One-liner:** Near-real-time, end-to-end information about shipment
location, status, and expected arrival — including disruption
anticipation.

## What it is

In [[fedex-viettel-post-partnership]], visibility is positioned as the
key technology layer behind the partnership: FedEx data from ~17m daily
shipments feeds analytics consumed via tools like FedEx Surround. AI is
explicitly framed as an "enabler" rather than a replacement of human
operations.

## How it works

Visibility systems pull shipment events from sort scans, vehicle
telematics, customs filings, and IoT sensors into a unified data plane.
Customers consume the data through APIs, web dashboards, or notifications.
The harder challenge is *anticipation*: forecasting disruptions from
weather, geopolitical, or operational signals — the niche FedEx Surround
addresses.

## Where it shows up

- In [[end-to-end-logistics]] as the data-layer requirement
- In [[cross-border-e-commerce]] as the customer-experience differentiator
- In [[traceability]] as the close cousin for compliance use-cases
