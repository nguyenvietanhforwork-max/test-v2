# Traceability

**One-liner:** The ability to verify a product's path through every
production and distribution node — a precondition for IUU compliance
and a benefit of digital end-to-end logistics.

## What it is

In [[vasep-12-billion-seafood-2026]], traceability databases tied to
vessel monitoring systems are the infrastructure VASEP wants government
to invest in. Without them, IUU compliance for exports to the EU and US
breaks down.

## How it works

A traceability system records identifiers and custody transitions at each
node: vessel/farm → port → processing plant → cold chain → exporter →
importer. Modern systems use barcodes/QR, vessel-monitoring AIS data,
and increasingly blockchain or shared ledgers. Real-time visibility tools
(see [[fedex-viettel-post-partnership]] on FedEx Surround) generalise the
pattern to cross-border logistics broadly.
