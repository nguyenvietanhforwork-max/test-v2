# Trade Defense

**One-liner:** Importer-country measures (anti-dumping, countervailing,
safeguards) and exporter-country toolkits to anticipate, contest, or
mitigate them.

## What it is

Per [[vasep-12-billion-seafood-2026]], trade-defence investigations are
listed alongside disease and labour shortage as a top sectoral pressure
for Vietnamese seafood exporters. VASEP asks the government to continue
supporting firms through these cases, plus parallel scrutiny on
environment, traceability, and forced-labour standards at destination.

## How it works

Anti-dumping cases impose duties on imports priced below fair value;
countervailing duties target subsidies; safeguards cap surges. For
Vietnam's seafood industry, US-bound shrimp and pangasius have repeatedly
faced such cases. Firms need legal and data capacity to respond to
questionnaires within tight deadlines.

## Where it shows up

- In [[seafood-export]] as a top destination-market risk
- In [[iuu-fishing]] as an overlapping non-tariff control
