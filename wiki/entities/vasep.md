# VASEP

**Kind:** organization (industry association)
**Also known as:** Vietnam Association of Seafood Exporters and Producers; Hiệp hội Chế biến và Xuất khẩu Thủy sản Việt Nam

## Profile

The umbrella industry association for Vietnam's seafood processing and
export sector. VASEP aggregates members across aquaculture, capture
fishing, processing, and export trade, and lobbies the central government
and ministries on policy and compliance frameworks.
