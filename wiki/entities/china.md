# China

**Kind:** place (sovereign state)
**Also known as:** PRC, Trung Quốc

## Profile

Vietnam's largest seafood destination market in Jan–Apr 2026 — 26.7%
share with +52.3% YoY growth, the fastest-growing among the top 15
destinations ([[vasep-12-billion-seafood-2026]]). Also one of the five
UN Security Council permanent members in Vietnam's bamboo-diplomacy
strategic-partner network ([[fdi-vietnam-fair-sustainable]]). FDI
contributor (including Hong Kong).
