# Government of Vietnam

**Kind:** organization (national government)
**Also known as:** Chính phủ Việt Nam

## Profile

National executive of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam. Across the
ingested corpus, it appears as: (i) author of the bamboo-diplomacy posture
and the 17-FTA network ([[fdi-vietnam-fair-sustainable]]); (ii) addressee
of VASEP's institutional and infrastructure asks ([[vasep-12-billion-
seafood-2026]]); and (iii) implicit regulator of customs and logistics in
the [[fedex-viettel-post-partnership]].
