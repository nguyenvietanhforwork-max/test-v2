# Viettel Post

**Kind:** organization (postal / logistics)
**Also known as:** VTP

## Profile

Nationwide Vietnamese postal and logistics company, originally a Viettel
Group subsidiary. Operates a robust domestic fleet, warehousing, and
last-mile network. Designated National Network Provider for FedEx in
Vietnam effective 26 April 2026 ([[fedex-viettel-post-partnership]]).
