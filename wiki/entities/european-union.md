# European Union

**Kind:** organization (supranational union)
**Also known as:** EU

## Profile

Destination market for Vietnamese seafood exports, gatekept by the IUU
("yellow card") regime. Appears in [[vasep-12-billion-seafood-2026]] as
the target market for Circulars 81/2025 and 74/2025 amendment requests.
